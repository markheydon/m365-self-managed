# M365 Self Managed

> A practical guide to running Microsoft 365 Business Premium yourself.

**IMPORTANT**: This project is still in its early stages. The content is incomplete and may change significantly over time.

## About

Many small businesses buy Microsoft 365 Business Premium because they know they should take security, compliance and device management seriously.

The problem is that most guidance assumes you have:

- A Microsoft partner.
- An MSP.
- An internal IT team.
- Years of Microsoft 365 experience.

Most small businesses have none of those things.

**M365 Self Managed** aims to bridge that gap by providing practical, step-by-step guidance that helps small businesses get the most from Microsoft 365 Business Premium without needing a consultant.

This resource is designed for:

- Business owners.
- Small teams.
- Start-ups.
- Charities.
- Organisations with 1 to 25 users.
- Anyone responsible for Microsoft 365 who isn't an IT professional.

## Goals

The goal is not to create a perfect enterprise environment.

The goal is to help small businesses implement a sensible, realistic and secure Microsoft 365 setup that:

- Improves security.
- Protects business data.
- Reduces risk.
- Follows good practice.
- Remains understandable and maintainable.

## Principles

This project is built around a few simple principles:

### Keep It Practical

Every recommendation should have a clear purpose and real-world benefit.

### Keep It Understandable

Guidance should be written for small business owners, not Microsoft consultants.

### Keep It Affordable

Recommendations should focus on capabilities already available within Microsoft 365 Business Premium wherever possible.

### Keep It Maintainable

The end result should be something a small business can realistically support themselves.
